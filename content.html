Stay calm and follow the fire emergency procedure:

  1. Activate the fire alarm in the glass case (red button), 2 are available on each floor.
  2. Call emergency number 85112, available 7/7 and 24/24.
  3. Try to put it out using the extinguishers available.
    1. Item 1
    2. Item 2
    3. Item 3
  4. Leave the building when the alarm sounds.
  5. Close all windows before leaving your office.
  6. Close the door but do not use the key.
  7. Follow the signs leading to the emergency.    
  8. If you find yourself stuck in a disaster, call 85112 and draw attention to yourself at the window.
  9. If there is smoke:
    • Keep low, as any fresh air will be near the ground (a wet cloth will help).
    • Keep your hand against the wall, so you are not disoriented by smoke.
    • Block openings in the doors, if possible with wet cloths, to prevent smoke from getting in.
  10. Do not use the lifts or escalators.
  11. Do not re-enter the building before receiving authorisation to do so.

Features

  • Holders of the credit card are personally liable
  • all payments are initiated by the cardholder.
  • The credit limit per calendar month is:
    • €2 500 for agents with grades 1 to 8 and for all contractual agents, detached national experts and accredited parliamentary assistants from grades 1 to 16; 
    • €4 000 for agents with grades 9 to 16 and for accredited parliamentary assistants from grades 17 to 19;
    • in exceptional, substantiated circumstances, however, the limits may be reviewed at the cardholder's request by email.(Open in new windows)
  • In Belgium, Luxembourg, the Netherlands, France, Germany, and Italy, direct debits are mandatory on bank accounts. For all other European countries where future cardholders may have a bank account, direct debits are not applicable and the full due amount needs to be paid manually by bank transfer.  
  • The Karen MCCLUSKEY : LUX - SCH 02A015 +352 4300 27137 karen.mccluskey@europarl.europa.eu EP Directory profile can reimburse withdrawal charges incurred on the credit card during a mission outside the euro zone - on the basis of the issuer's invoice - when mission expenses are settled.
  • Exchange rates are based on the daily interbank exchange rate, with a 1.75% mark-up to cover the card issuer's costs.
